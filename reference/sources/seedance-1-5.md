## Key Specifications Verified

- **Resolution**: 480p / 720p / 1080p @ 24fps (verified across 5+ sources)
- **Duration**: 4-12 seconds (verified across 4+ sources)
- **Aspect Ratios**: 16:9, 9:16, 1:1, 4:3, 3:4, 21:9 (verified across 3+ sources)
- **Input Modes**: Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video, Video Extend (verified across 4+ sources)
- **Audio**: Native joint generation — dialogue, SFX, ambient sound (verified across 6+ sources)
- **Multi-language Lip Sync**: English, Mandarin, Japanese, Korean, Spanish, Indonesian, Sichuan dialect, Shaanxi dialect

## Unique Differentiators

1. **Native audio-video joint generation** — single inference produces both video frames and audio waveforms
2. **Multi-language lip sync** — supports 8+ languages including Chinese dialects
3. **Dual-Branch DiT architecture** — separate video/audio branches with attention-based fusion
4. **10x inference acceleration** — multi-stage distillation + quantization
5. **Video Extend** — continue existing videos with motion/aesthetic continuity
