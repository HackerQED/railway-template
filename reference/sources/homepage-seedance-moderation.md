# Seedance 2.0 Content Moderation & Human Face Research

## Key Findings

### Face Moderation: Three-Layer System
1. **Prompt text filter** — keyword pattern matching (English stricter than Chinese)
2. **Face upload filter** — CV detection scans for photorealistic human faces
3. **Post-generation filter** — output scanned again, violations rejected

### Rejection Rate
- Real photos: ~100% blocked
- AI-generated realistic faces: frequently blocked (false positives)
- Helmets/glasses/sunglasses: still blocked
- Side angles, partial occlusion: may be blocked
- Stylized/cartoon faces: sometimes false positives

### Official Statement
Dreamina operations: "Seedance 2.0 attracted far more attention than expected during internal testing... currently does not support inputting real human images or videos as subject references."
